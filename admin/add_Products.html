The product code can be any combination of letters and numbers.
In case you have a product with different colors and sizes, we suggest that you set up the same product code.
For instance, a white T-shirt and back T-shirt of the same brand and model can be under the same product code as it will ease your clients to browse for variation of your products in your store.
